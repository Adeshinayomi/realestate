About Progress Estates

We understand that every area, every property and every client is different - one size does not fit all. So while many agents focus purely on their sales processes and meeting targets, Evolution Estates as an Independent Estate agent also focuses on people and delivering a truly excellent service to each and every one of its clients. If you are searching for an Estate Agent to serve you with commitment, passion and energy, an agency that offers a combination of local expertise and knowledge of the property market, an experienced team that will handle the sale of your home with the greatest of trust, look no further.
